How It Works

From Idea to Video in 4 Steps

Everything happens fast — your Vercel backend handles the heavy lifting.

1

Type Your Prompt

Describe your video in plain language. Include product, tone, audience. The more detail the better the script.

2

Vercel Calls Gemini AI

Your Vercel serverless function calls Google Gemini API to generate a full professional script with scene keywords. Takes 3–5 seconds.

3

HD Clips Are Fetched

Vercel fetches matching HD video clips from Pexels based on the script keywords. Takes 5–10 seconds depending on your connection.

4

Video Plays with Voiceover

HD clips play in sequence in your browser while the AI-generated script is read aloud. Click download to save the video clips.

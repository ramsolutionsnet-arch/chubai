How It Works

From Idea to Video in 4 Steps

Your Vercel backend handles everything. You just type and watch.

1

Type Your Prompt

Describe your video. Include the product, mood, audience, and duration. The more detail the better the result.

2

Gemini Writes the Script

Your Vercel function calls Google Gemini API to write a full narration script with scene keywords. Takes 3–5 seconds.

3

Pexels Fetches HD Clips

Vercel fetches matching HD video clips from Pexels based on the script keywords. Takes 5–8 seconds.

4

Video Plays with Voiceover

Clips play in your browser while your selected language voice reads the AI script aloud. Click download to save!
